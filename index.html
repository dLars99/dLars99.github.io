A little about me

I have been fascinated by software programs since I was very young. I learned fundamentals in BASIC and C++. However, I set that aside in pursuit of a musical career. Now, work in tech support rekindled my interest in coding. I used some self-study to start catching up, but realized I would need more to pursue this professionally. That led me to Nashville Software School, who gave me the opportunity to go back down the "road not taken." The skills I have learned allow me to work logically and creatively to build meaningful, fulfilling projects.
